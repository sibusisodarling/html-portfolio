The Best Movies According to Darlington

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Bourne Identity

This is my favourite Action movie of all time. I love the the way the movie builds up.
Unlike the flashy and gadget-heavy Bond films of the time, The Bourne Identity took a more grounded,
realistic approach. It emphasized raw, tactical intelligence and physicality over tech and glamor.
That felt fresh and more relatable.

My Cousin Vinny

The humor in My Cousin Vinny comes from character-driven situations, not cheap gags.
Joe Pesci’s fast-talking Vinny and Marisa Tomei’s no-nonsense Mona Lisa Vito are hilarious, but never cartoonish.
The comedy feels natural, even during high-stakes courtroom moments.

The Bourne Ultimatum

The Bourne Ultimatum (2007) is often hailed as the best entry in the Bourne series—and one of the greatest modern
action thrillers—for several compelling reasons. It brought together everything that made the first two films work
and elevated them to a peak level of execution. From the opening frame, The Bourne Ultimatum never slows down.
The film moves with surgical precision—jumping from one location to another with urgency but never confusion.
The editing (which won an Oscar) keeps the tension high without sacrificing clarity.
